# Tokyo Guide

## Base Neighborhood: Nihonbashi / Hatchobori

### Why This Area

The family's planned Tokyo destinations span east and west:

| Destination | Travel from Nihonbashi | Notes |
|-------------|----------------------|-------|
| Ginza | 10–15 min walk or 1 stop | Essentially free |
| Tokyo Station | 10 min walk | Shinkansen to Hakone on Jun 2 |
| Shinjuku | ~30 min by subway | Direct line |
| Shibuya / Harajuku | ~30 min by subway | Direct line |
| Disneyland / DisneySea | ~30 min via JR Keiyo Line | From Tokyo Station, no transfer |

Nihonbashi/Hatchobori sits at the center of all these directions, making it the most efficient base. The area itself is quiet, local, and not overrun with tourists — a welcome contrast to staying in Shinjuku or Shibuya directly.

## Getting Around Tokyo

**Mode:** Mix of subway + taxis/Uber

- Get a **Suica or Pasmo IC card** at the airport — works on all subway, JR, and most buses
- **Taxis** are clean, safe, and metered — good for late nights or when kids are tired
- **Uber** works in Tokyo — useful to have the app set up before arrival
- For DisneySea/Disneyland: take the **JR Keiyo Line** from Tokyo Station to Maihama Station (~15 min, very straightforward)

## Planned Destinations

### Shibuya / Harajuku
- Takeshita Street for kids (colorful, fun food)
- Meiji Shrine (peaceful, wooded, great for kids to run around)
- Shibuya crossing (iconic, but overwhelming with young kids — best early morning)

### Shinjuku
- Shinjuku Gyoen garden (beautiful in late May/early June)
- Kabukicho robot restaurant or themed cafes
- Department store food halls (depachika) — excellent for a family meal

### DisneySea / Disneyland
- Both parks are in Urayasu, Chiba — ~30 min from Tokyo Station
- **DisneySea** is widely considered one of the best theme parks in the world
- Book FastPass/Premier Access in advance via the Tokyo Disney Resort app
- Arrive early — gates open at 8am, most rides are walkable before 10am

### Ginza / Tokyo Station area
- Ginza Six (shopping)
- Tsukiji Outer Market (10 min from Ginza — excellent breakfast/brunch)
- Tokyo Station itself is worth exploring (Ramen Street, Character Street underground)
- teamLab Planets is in Toyosu (~20 min from Ginza) — book tickets well in advance, incredible for kids
