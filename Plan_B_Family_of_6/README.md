# 🇯🇵 Japan Trip 2026

A family trip to Japan for 6 people — 2 adults, 2 kids (ages 7 & 9), and grandma (75, active & healthy).

## 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦 The Group

| Traveler | Notes |
|---|---|
| Mom (trip planner) | Culture, food, onsen, shopping |
| Dad | Tech, gadgets, cool experiences |
| Kid 1 (age 9) | Pokémon, Disney, adventure |
| Kid 2 (age 7) | Pokémon, Disney, animals |
| Grandma (age 75) | Comfort, culture, relaxation, nice hotels |

## 📅 Trip Dates
- **Duration:** 10 days
- **Season:** Early June 2026
- **Departure:** San Francisco (SFO)
- **Arrival:** Tokyo Haneda (HND)

## 🗺️ Cities
1. **Tokyo** — 5 nights
2. **Hakone** — 2 nights
3. **Kyoto** — 3 nights

## ✈️ Recommended Flights
- **Airline:** JAL (Japan Airlines) or ANA — nonstop SFO → HND
- **Why JAL/ANA over budget carriers:** Full meals, seatback screens, family-friendly service, better seats for a 12-hr flight with kids and grandma
- See [`logistics/flights.md`](logistics/flights.md) for full comparison
