## Why DisneySea is Special

Tokyo DisneySea is widely considered the best theme park in the world — and it's a very different experience from US Disney parks. The theming, storytelling, food, and atmosphere are extraordinarily immersive. Even adults without kids feel the magic. June 2026 is a great time to visit as the park's **25th Anniversary "Sparkling Jubilee"** celebration will be in full swing.

## Getting There

**From Shinjuku (Hyatt Regency):**
- JR Keiyo Line: Shinjuku → Maihama Station (~50 min)
- Walk or monorail to DisneySea (~5–10 min from Maihama)
- Total door-to-door: ~60 min
- Cost: ~¥1,000/person (Suica)

**Alternative (easier for grandma):**
- Taxi from hotel: ~45–60 min, ~¥8,000–12,000 depending on traffic
- Book through GO app the night before

**Depart hotel:** No later than 8:00am to arrive at park opening (9:00am)

## Tickets

- **Buy in advance** at [tokyodisneyresort.jp](https://www.tokyodisneyresort.jp) — they sell out
- Ticket prices vary by date (value/regular/peak) — June is typically regular pricing
- Approximate cost: ~¥7,900–10,900/adult, ~¥5,200–7,400/child (check current prices)
- Download **Tokyo Disney Resort App** before purchasing — everything is managed through it

## Strategy for a Day at DisneySea

### Timing
- **Arrive at 8:30am** (park opens 9am — lines form early)
- First 2 hours: tackle your must-dos before crowds build
- After 3pm: lines often shorter as day visitors leave
- **Stay for the evening show** — DisneySea's evening harbor shows are legendary

### Premier Access (Paid Skip-the-Line)
- Available through the app for specific rides
- Purchase as soon as the park opens (some go fast)
- Priority picks: Journey to the Center of the Earth, Soaring, Raging Spirits
- Cost: ~¥1,500–2,000 per person per attraction

### Split-Group Strategy
While one adult rides thrill rides with the older/braver kid, the other adult can:
- Take the younger kid on Mermaid Lagoon attractions
- Sit with grandma for shows
- Grab food and meet at a designated spot

### Grandma Tips
- **Wheelchair rental** available at the main entrance — consider for long days
- Companion restrooms available throughout park
- All major attractions have accessibility options
- The Mediterranean Harbor area has many benches and beautiful views — a perfect base
- Use the **Disney app** to check wait times and plan the next move without walking unnecessarily
