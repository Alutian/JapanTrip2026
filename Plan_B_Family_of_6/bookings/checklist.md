## 📋 Pre-Departure Checklist

### Documents
- [ ] All 6 passports valid for 6+ months beyond return date
- [ ] JR Passes received and packed (don't activate yet)
- [ ] All hotel confirmations downloaded / printed
- [ ] All activity tickets saved to phone (screenshot + email)
- [ ] Travel insurance policy documents
- [ ] Grandma's doctor letter for medications
- [ ] Emergency contact card for each person (with hotel addresses in Japanese)

### Finance
- [ ] Notify all banks/credit cards of Japan travel
- [ ] Get some yen cash (~$200–300 equivalent per adult)
- [ ] Confirm cards work internationally (no foreign transaction fees ideally)
- [ ] Set up 7-Eleven ATM plan for cash top-ups in Japan

### Apps (Download Before Departure)
- [ ] Google Maps (offline: Tokyo, Kyoto, Hakone)
- [ ] Google Translate (offline Japanese)
- [ ] Tokyo Disney Resort App
- [ ] PokéPark KANTO App
- [ ] teamLab App
- [ ] GO (taxi app)

### Health
- [ ] Doctor consultation for grandma — confirm fitness for travel
- [ ] All prescriptions filled with enough supply + extra
- [ ] Kids' vaccinations up to date
- [ ] Pack first aid kit
