# ✈️ Flights

**Route:** San Francisco (SFO) → Tokyo Haneda (HND) → San Francisco (SFO)
**Travel dates:** Early June 2026 (~10 days)
**Passengers:** 4 adults (including grandma, 75) + 2 children (ages 7 & 9)

---

## Recommended Airlines

### 1. JAL (Japan Airlines) ⭐ Top Pick for This Group

- **Route:** SFO → HND (nonstop, ~11–12 hours)
- Full-service carrier — meals, drinks, seatback entertainment included
- Seatback screens with excellent kids' content
- Legendary Japanese cabin service
- 2-4-2 seating in economy — family of 6 can sit together as a block
- Best on-time performance on transpacific routes (88% on schedule)
- **Flexible tickets** — changes allowed (important when traveling with kids/elderly)
- Book at [jal.co.jp](https://www.jal.co.jp/en/) or Google Flights

### 2. ANA (All Nippon Airways) ⭐ Close Second

- **Route:** SFO → HND (nonstop, ~11–12 hours)
- Economy seats: 34-inch pitch (3 extra inches vs. US carriers)
- Excellent meal service, kids' amenity kits
- Comparable to JAL in quality; often slightly different pricing
- Book at [ana.co.jp](https://www.ana.co.jp/en/us/) or Google Flights

### 3. United Airlines

- Nonstop SFO → HND and SFO → NRT
- Convenient if you have United miles or status
- In-flight experience on transpacific lags behind JAL/ANA
- Economy more cramped; food quality lower
- Use if significantly cheaper or points redemption makes sense

### Why Not ZIPAIR (JAL's Budget Subsidiary)
| Factor | ZIPAIR | JAL/ANA |
|--------|--------|---------|
| Price | Lower base fare | Higher, all-in |
| Bags (1 per person) | $59/bag each way = $472–708 RT for 6 | Included |
| Meals | Pay per item | Included |
| Seats | 31" pitch, 3-3-3 layout | 33-34", 2-4-2 layout |
| Entertainment | Bring your own device | Seatback screens |
| Flexibility | Non-refundable | Changes allowed |
| Kids 7+ discount | None | Modest |

**Verdict:** Once you add bags (6 people, 9 days), food, and seat selection, ZIPAIR's price advantage mostly disappears — and you lose the comfort, flexibility, and family-friendly features that matter on a 12-hour flight with kids and grandma.

## Booking Strategy

### Where to Search
- **[Google Flights](https://flights.google.com)** — Best overall. Use the price calendar view. Set alerts.
- **[JAL direct](https://www.jal.co.jp/en/)** — Sometimes has deals not on aggregators
- **[ANA direct](https://www.ana.co.jp/en/us/)** — Same
- **[Kayak](https://kayak.com)** — Good for side-by-side comparison
- **[Costco Travel](https://travel.costco.com)** — Often excellent Japan packages with added perks

### Best Dates to Target
- Depart SFO: **June 1–5** (avoids any Golden Week premium)
- Return SFO: **June 10–15**
- Midweek departures (Tue/Wed) typically cheapest

### Price Expectations (Economy, per person round-trip)
| Airline | Estimated Range |
|---------|----------------|
| JAL/ANA | $1,000–$1,600 |
| United | $850–$1,300 |
| **Total for 6 (JAL/ANA)** | **$6,000–$9,600** |

> Prices fluctuate. Book 3–4 months in advance for best availability. June is shoulder season (post-Golden Week, pre-peak summer) — you're in a good window.

### Seat Selection Tips
- Book seats as early as possible after purchasing
- For 6 people on a 2-4-2 layout: book 4 middle seats + 2 aisle seats in same row
- Kids sit in the middle block with one parent; grandma gets an aisle
- Consider **Premium Economy** for grandma — extra legroom and recline is meaningful on a 12-hour flight for a 75-year-old

## Airport Notes

### Departing SFO
- **Terminal:** International Terminal
- Arrive **3 hours before departure** for international flights
- TSA PreCheck/Global Entry recommended if you have it
- SFO has good dining and gate areas — build in time

### Arriving HND (Tokyo Haneda)
- **Much better than Narita** for families — closer to central Tokyo
- Immigration can take 30–60 min — be patient
- **Suica IC cards:** Purchase at the airport (vending machines in arrivals) for all 6 people
- Transport to Shinjuku:
  - **Keikyu Line** → Shinjuku (~30 min, ~¥600/person) — cheapest
  - **Limousine Bus** → Shinjuku (~45–60 min, ~¥1,300/person) — more comfortable with luggage, grandma-friendly
  - **Taxi** (~60–90 min, ~¥8,000–15,000 depending on traffic) — for 6 people with luggage, split across 2 taxis

### Returning from HND
- Arrive **3 hours before departure**
- Excellent duty-free shopping in the international terminal
- **Edo Market** in departures — best airport souvenir shopping in Japan

## Packing for the Flight

### For Kids (7 & 9)
- Loaded iPads/tablets with downloaded movies and games (ZIPAIR has no screens; JAL/ANA do, but backup is smart)
- Headphones
- Small travel pillow
- A few new small toys/activities as a "surprise" for the flight
- Snacks from home (Japanese airline food is good but familiar snacks help)

### For Grandma
- Compression socks for the flight (reduces swelling, important for 12 hours)
- Neck pillow
- Eye mask
- Aisle seat booked — easy bathroom access
- Consider Premium Economy upgrade for her comfort

### For Everyone
- **Download before you board:** Google Maps offline (Tokyo, Hakone, Kyoto), translation app, Disney Resort app, teamLab app
- Melatonin for jet lag management
- Light layers — plane is cold
