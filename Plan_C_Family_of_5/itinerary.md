## Day 7 — Thu Jun 4 · Travel to Kyoto + Fushimi Inari Evening

🔒 **LOCKED**
- Takkyubin Hakone → Kyoto (morning handoff)

- Shinkansen Odawara → Kyoto (~1h45, reserved seats)

🎯 **FLEXIBLE** — pick by energy

> Decide on the Shinkansen based on how the family is feeling.
