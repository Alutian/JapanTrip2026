# Narita City Menu

One night only (June 7). Goal: pleasant low-stress last evening, 5:30am taxi for grandma the next morning.

---

## 🌅 Afternoon (arriving ~19:00 from Kyoto transit)

Everyone's tired from travel. Check in, quick freshen up, head out for dinner. Not a sightseeing slot.

---

## 🌃 Evening Blocks

### NAR1 · Omotesando + Unagi Farewell Dinner ⭐
- **Area:** Narita City, Omotesando Street (10-min walk from JR Narita Station)
- **Activity:** Walk the 800m old pilgrimage road to Naritasan Shinshoji. Wooden shops, sweet stalls, festival atmosphere. If any energy — **Naritasan temple complex at night** is genuinely pretty (illuminated pagoda, large park).
- **Food:** **Kawatoyo Honten** ⭐ — the famous unagi spot. They butterfly live eels on the street window out front (kids are fascinated or horrified). 100+ year old shop, always a queue; arrive by 18:30. Una-ju (eel over rice) is the order. Expect ~¥4,500/person.
- **Alternative:** **Kikuya** — equally historic unagi, slightly less queue, similar price.
- **Vibe:** The last real Japan meal. Worth the queue.

### NAR2 · Airport-Area Izakaya (backup)
- If the family is wiped and doesn't want the Omotesando walk, most Narita City business hotels have izakayas within 5 min. Nothing exceptional, but **Gyu-Kaku** (yakiniku chain) is reliable for kids.

## 🌄 Departure Morning (June 8)

### Grandma — 05:30 Taxi to NRT
- Pre-book the taxi the night before via hotel reception (they all do this routinely for 5am runs).
- 10-min ride, ~¥2,500.
- For **08:20 NRT → SIN departure:** at terminal by 06:20 = 2h pre-flight. ZIPAIR check-in closes 1h pre, other airlines vary.

### Family — Relaxed Morning
- **Hotel breakfast**, packing
- **Optional ~09:00:** short walk into Naritasan Park / temple grounds in daylight — it's genuinely worth 30 min in a park-walk sense (not a temple-visit sense)
- **Early light lunch:** unagi leftover? or konbini bento for the plane
- **12:30:** check out, taxi or Keisei line to NRT (10 min, ~¥2,500 taxi / ¥260 Keisei)
- **13:30:** at NRT for 16:25 ZG030

## 🏨 Hotel Context

For this one night, two viable zones:

1. **Narita City center** (near JR Narita / Keisei Narita stations, Omotesando walkable) — Richmond Hotel, Hotel Welco Narita, Mercure Narita. ~¥12,000–20,000/night, 10 min taxi to NRT.
2. **Airport-area hotels** (Hilton, Nikko, Marroad) — shuttle to terminal, but Omotesando requires a 15-min taxi back into town. Only pick if family wants zero movement.

**Recommendation:** City-center zone. The unagi dinner justifies it.

---

## 🚫 Skip

- Eating at the airport the night before — we've come this far, don't eat Wagamama at NRT on the last night
- Airport-district hotels unless the family is done and just wants to collapse near the terminal
